# FUTURE BUSINESS FEATURE ROADMAP

This roadmap details the planned business capabilities to be added to the eCommerce platform.

## 1. Planned Business Features

* **Coupons & Promotions Subsystem**: Supports coupon validation and cart discounts calculations.
  * *Business Value*: Drives marketing campaigns, increases sales.
  * *Complexity*: Medium.
  * *Priority*: **HIGH** (Target: Sprint 15).
* **Product Reviews & Ratings**: Allows customers to write reviews and rate products.
  * *Business Value*: Increases customer trust and engagement.
  * *Complexity*: Low.
  * *Priority*: **MEDIUM** (Target: Sprint 16).
* **Wishlist**: Allows customers to save products for later purchase.
  * *Business Value*: Retains customers, increases cart conversion rates.
  * *Complexity*: Low.
  * *Priority*: **MEDIUM** (Target: Sprint 16).
